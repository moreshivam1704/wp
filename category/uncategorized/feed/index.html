About UPI

UPI stands for unified payment interface (UPI). It allows transfer of money from one bank account to another account instantly via one’s mobile phone. Payments can be made via app on mobile device only. There are various applications based on UPI payment some are Google Pay,Phonepe,BHIM UPI and Paytm.

 It runs by registered phone number to the bank account anyone easily access the payment within few seconds. The money transfer limit per day is 1 lakh Rupees. After 24 hours it rests.

 

Money Transfer Charges In India

The application which is based on UPI money transfer dosen’t take any charges government of india taking charges on NEFT and RTGS but new law was passed on November 2020 government has passed a law there was no charges on UPI transactions and have 2.4 percent charges on RTGS.

 

 The third party applications was not allowed to take any extra charges on the payment by users this charge list may changed or update by time.
